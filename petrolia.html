Our Story

Petrolia Liquor Store’s journey began in December of 1993 when four friends came together to venture into the liquor store business. The business was not just about the four partners but more about the families of these partners and the community. The support of the community is what makes Petrolia Liquor Store what it is today. The establishment was later passed down to the children of these four friends and continues to be a family-owned business. It is through this store that these four friends became a family that will continue on for many generations to come. Here at Petrolia, we strive to treat each customer with warm and personalized service. It is through our store’s product selection, competitive pricing, and friendly customer service that our business continues to evolve.
